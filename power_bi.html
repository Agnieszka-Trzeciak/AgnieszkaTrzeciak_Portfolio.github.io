Endangered species of US national parks

Project Overwiev

Dashboard focused on visualizing threatened, endangered and recovering species, that are present in national parks in the US. It gives the viewer information including the number of individual species, how common they are within parks, and their conservation status. Using the dashboard, it's possible to find out which states' parks hold most of United States' endangered species.

Data source: Kaggle, Biodiversity in National Parks
Dashboard limited to sharing using images due to limitations of license free version of MS Power BI

Dashboard Interactivity

Filtering

Providing options to filter the displayed information to just ones relevant to the viewer gives them an option to easily access information about only a portion of overall data. Thanks to MS Power BI functionality the whole dashboard updates to present just the requested data.

Drilling down

Including drill down allows to hide additional information and filter functionality within a dashboard. This allows charts to change their use or provide more specific information. By efficiently using all three MS Power BI drill down options, and providing plentiful information within graphs, a data analyst can quickly access data during a presentation.
